Event Description

NSU MIBC proudly unveils the nation's first-ever International Business based case competition - Smart Technologies Presents INNOVADE 2026, Driven by Yamaha, Co-Sponsored by Polar Ice Cream, and Powered by NOVOAIR.

Designed to push boundaries, INNOVADE 2026 immerses participants in real-world international business challenges, fostering strategic acumen and global leadership.

Your moment to INNOVADE is here.

Round 1: TradeForge

An online international business case-solving round where teams analyze real-world global challenges and propose strategic, data-driven solutions.

Semi-Final: StrategyCraft

Shortlisted teams design and present strategic policy-driven solutions through a professional poster, addressing global business and regulatory challenges.

Final Round: GlobalPitch

Finalist teams deliver high-impact live presentations, defending innovative global strategies before a panel of industry experts.
